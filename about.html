About Us

IndiGo is India’s largest and most preferred passenger airline and amongst the fastest-growing airlines in the world.We have a simple philosophy: offer fares that are affordable, flights that are on time, and provide a courteous and hassle-free travel experience across our unparalleled network. We demonstrate that low cost does not mean low quality. With our fleet of 400+ aircraft, we operate well over 2,300 daily flights, connecting 130+ destinations (of which 40+ are international), welcoming 118 million+ customers on board last year. We have an industry-leading on-time performance and one of the highest customer NPS in the Indian market. At IndiGo, we will continue to extend our scope, by spreading our wings internationally, from a domestic carrier to a global aviation leader.Right from IndiGo’s start, it has been our mission to connect the vast and diverse India, supporting social cohesion, mobility, and economic progress. With our unparalleled network, we are giving wings to the nation, enabling air travel to all large, medium, and smaller cities across India. This is also briefly captured as ‘India by IndiGo’.

Mission

IndiGo's mission is "Giving wings to the nation, by connecting people and aspirations," which translates to providing affordable, hassle-free, and on-time air travel across India's vast and diverse regions, including smaller cities, to foster social cohesion, economic progress, and mobility

History

IndiGo Airlines was founded in 2005 by Rahul Bhatia and Rakesh Gangwal and began operations on August 4, 2006. Headquartered in Gurugram, it is India's largest airline by passenger market share, operating as a low-cost carrier focused on keeping costs low to provide affordable fares and on-time flights. The airline went public in October 2015 and, through a strategy of rapid fleet expansion and operational efficiency, achieved profitability early on, even as other Indian airlines struggled.InterGlobe Aviation Limited, doing business as IndiGo, is an Indian airline headquartered in Gurgaon.Its primary hub is at the Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi.

Vision

IndiGo's vision is to be a global aviation giant, recognized for safe, on-time, and hassle-free travel experiences, by becoming the most preferred and profitable airline in the world through operational efficiency, a customer-centric approach, a focus on innovation and technology, and sustainable growth, while serving India's economic progress by providing affordable and accessible air travel.
