BagPack

A backpack, also called knapsack, schoolbag, rucksack, pack, booksack, bookbag, haversack, packsack, or backsack, is in its simplest frameless form, a fabric sack carried on one's back and secured with two straps that go over the shoulders; but it can have an external or internal frame, and there are bodypacks.

Food

Airline meals vary from snacks to gourmet dinners depending on the airline, class, and flight length, with food typically prepared in ground-based catering facilities and reheated on board to account for the dulling of taste buds at high altitudes. While some airlines provide complimentary meals on longer flights, many offer food for purchase, often featuring local cuisine and specially seasoned dishes to counteract the effects of cabin pressure.

Staff

Aeroplane staff includes the flight crew, such as pilots and cabin crew (flight attendants), who operate the aircraft and ensure passenger safety and comfort, and ground staff at airports, including mechanics, baggage handlers, air traffic controllers, and check-in agents, who maintain the aircraft and manage airport operations
